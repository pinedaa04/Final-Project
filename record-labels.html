Fearless Records

Fearles Records was founded in 1991 in Culver City, California by Bob Becker. Bob started Fearless when he was selling tacos at shows. He would go around record stores and put their records on consignment. He based the name of the record lable on his moto of "not being afraid to jump head first." Fearless is known for their Punk Goes... series which takes songs of different genres and has punk bands cover them.

Fueled by Ramen

Fueled by Ramen is an American record label, which is currently owned by Warner Music Group. The label was founded in Gainesville, Florida in the year 1996 by John Janick. Janick thought of starting a record label while he attended high school. He started the label while he was attending the University of Florida. He teamed up with Vinne Florello, the drummer from the band Less Than Jake, to make his record label dreams a reality. The name of the band was inspirted by Florello and Janick's experinece in only affording cheap meals such as ramen during the beginning of the growth of the record label. To many fans of FBR bands, FBR is a lifestyle.

Hopeless Records

Hopeless Records is an American Independent record label. It was founded in 1993 in Van Nuys, California by Louis Posen. The genres that most artists at Hopeless are punk rock, pop punk, post-hardcore and alternative rock. Through out its lifetime, Hopeless has helped many bands make their dreams a reality. Not only do they help bands they also help the community with their charitabel arm, Sub City Records.
